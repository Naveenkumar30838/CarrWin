    • What is Nimcet Exam

    The National Institute of Technology Master of Computer Applications Common Entrance Test (NIMCET) is a national level entrance exam conducted by the NITs. This entrance exam is conducted every year for interested graduates to be offered admission into Masters in Computer Application (MCA)Programme in the participating NITs . Candidates qualifying for the NIMCET 2023 exam with a good rank, will be allotted seats at various campuses of NITs on the basis of their ranks. Every year the number of applications received for the NIMCET exam is around 50,000 students whereas approximately 805 seats are offered in 11 NITs (Number vary year to year) across India. Many universities and institutes other than NITs consider NIMCET scores for admissions to the MCA programmes offered by them.

    Nimcet Eligibility Criteria

    The NIMCET Eligibility Criteria are prescribed by NITs responsible for conducting the exam. The candidate must satisfy the eligibility requirement prescribed by the official authorities to appear in the exam. The detailed information about NIMCET 2023 eligibility has been given below-

    Education Qualification :

    1. Those candidates must have any degree with mathematics/ statistics for a minimum of 3 years full time or B.E / B.Tech or some equivalent exam from a recognized University will be able to apply for the NIMCET 2023 examination.
    2. Candidates who have completed their B.Sc / B.Sc (Hons)/ BCA/ BIT for 3 years from Open Universities will also be able to apply for the examination.

    Appearing :

    Candidates who are appearing in the qualifying exam will also be able to apply for the examination.

    Minimum Marks Required :

    Indian National Applicants required to have attained at least 60% as aggregate or 6.5/10 CGPA and 55% marks or 6.0/10 CGPA for SC, ST will also be applied for the examination.

    Age :

    There is no age limit to apply for NIMCET. Candidates of any age can apply and appear in the NIMCETs exam.

    Nimcet Exam Pattern

    The NIMCET exam pattern describes the type and pattern of questions to be asked in the upcoming entrance exam. NIMCET exam is conducted in online (CBT) mode. The question paper will be in English language only and will contain 120 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQ) of maximum 480 Marks in total.Time given for Attempting the paper is 2 hours

  • 4 marks will be awarded for each correct answer.
  • -1 mark will be deducted for every wrong answer.
  • The question paper will be divided into 4 sections. Each section contains different marks sections

    Nimcet Application Fee

    Candidates are required to pay the requisite NIMCET application fee online via Net-banking/Credit Card/Debit Card/Mobile wallets. The service charge (as applicable) will have to be borne by the candidate. Every applicant must keep in mind that the NIMCET application fee is non-refundable and non-transferable under any circumstances.

    Nimcet Councelling

    Candidates willing to participate in the NIMCET Counselling process will have to apply online mode. The NIMCET Counselling is Usually held in June Month. Seats will be allotted on the basis of the rank scored by the candidates, preferred choices filled by the candidates, and availability of seats. Only those candidates who are successful in qualifying for the entrance exam will be permitted to fill in the choices of the institution where they are seeking admission. Candidates need to carry the below-listed documents with them for the counselling process.

  • 1. Mark sheets and certificates of 10thand 12th
  • 2. Residence Certificate
  • 3. Transfer Certificate
  • 4. Income Certificate
  • 5. Caste Certificate
  • 6. Provisional Admission Letter
  • 7. NIMCET Score Card

    • How to fill Nimcet Application Form

    1. Open the official website

    2. Click on the "NIMCET 2024 Registration" link

    3. Fill the required details to complete the registration process

    4. After registration, fill the application form with the required information

    5. Upload scanned image of photo and signature

    6. Pay application fees online using a credit/debit card or internet banking

    The application form will be submitted automatically after the successful payment of the application fees. A PDF of the form will be generated after final submission. Candidates need to download the PDF and take its printout.

    NIMCET Application Form Correction

    The facility to make corrections in the NIMCET 2024 application will be opened after the completion of the registration process. Candidates need to login to the official website of NIMCET 2024 for making the corrections.

    Why NIMCET ?

    1. Admission to top NITs : The NIMCET exam is the gateway to admission to the MCA program offered by some of the top NITs in India. These NITs are known for their excellent academic reputation and placements.

    2. High-paying jobs : MCA graduates from top NITs are in high demand among top IT companies. IT industry is one of the fastest-growing industries in the world, and there is a huge demand for skilled professionals. If you are passionate about computer science and want to pursue a career in the IT industry, then the NIMCET exam is a great way to start.

    3. Bright career prospects : The MCA degree is a gateway to a variety of exciting and rewarding careers in the IT industry. MCA graduates can work as software developers, data scientists, web developers, mobile app developers,System analysts, Business analysts , Project managers , IT consultants and more.

    4. Exposure to the latest technologies : NITs offer state-of-the-art facilities and infrastructure to their students. MCA students at NITs get the opportunity to learn about the latest technologies and trends in the IT industry.

    5. Strong alumni network : NITs have a strong alumni network that can be beneficial for MCA graduates in terms of job placement and career guidance.
