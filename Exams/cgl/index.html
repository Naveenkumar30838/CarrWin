About SSC CGL Exam

The Staff Selection Commission Combined Graduate Level (SSC CGL) exam is a highly competitive examination conducted by the SSC. It serves as a gateway to various Group B and Group C government jobs in India, spanning a wide range of departments and ministries. This examination is one of the most anticipated and sought-after in the country, offering candidates a chance to secure a stable and rewarding career in the public sector.

Paper Pattern

The SSC CGL exam consists of four tiers: Tier-I (Preliminary), Tier-II (Mains), Tier-III (Descriptive Paper), and Tier-IV (Skill Test/Computer Proficiency Test).
